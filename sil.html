Leadership & Innovation

Putting my Values In Action, I led the initiative of planning and execution of my school’s visit to AWWA ( Asian Women’s Welfare Association), working towards empowering persons with disabilities, families and vulnerable seniors. I managed the communication with the Organization, coordinating between them and my School, ranging from the timelines, schedule as well as the agenda of the visit. The visit created a lasting impact on me, understanding and appreciating the efforts of people with disabilities. It further inspired me to develop accessible devices and software that would help such people in the future. I was lauded by my teachers for the leadership potential I displayed and it has motivated me to keep working on this in the next steps of my career.

This project helped me TO DISCOVER a part of me that I never knew about myself, which was the passion to contribute back to society as a sign of gratefulness to what i have been provided with.

I guided my team through the initial communication progress, encouraged collaboration, and ensured we met our goals with quality results. This helped me to hone my communication, teamwork and coordination skills
