Who I Am

I’m Allen Hussain, a passionate tech enthusiast skilled in programming, problem-solving, and innovation. I’ve honed my abilities in Python, robotics, and web development, competing nationally at the National Youth Tech Championship—placing Top 8 in 2023 and Top 4 in 2024. Leading my Student-Initiated Learning project taught me valuable lessons in project management and teamwork.

Outside of tech, I value self-improvement and creativity. I believe in the power of consistency and continuous learning, and I bring that mindset to every challenge I take on.

Beyond tech, I’m an athlete who enjoys basketball and volleyball. Though my school doesn’t offer CCAs for these sports, I play casually and have competed in events like the Rampage 3x3 street basketball tournament. While we didn’t place at the top, I gained experience working under pressure, making quick decisions, and communicating effectively with teammates. I’ve also earned a bronze medal in last year’s badminton interclass and a gold medal in this year’s volleyball interclass cup division.
